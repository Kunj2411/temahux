## 🎨 Design System Highlights

### Color Palette
- Primary: #440CE3 (Purple)
- Accent: #DEC178 (Gold)
- Surface: #151022 (Dark)
- Text Muted: #9CA3AF (Gray)

### Spacing Scale
- Card padding: p-6 (24px)

- Hero: text-5xl md:text-8xl

- Body: text-sm/text-base

- Duration: 300ms
- Easing: Default
- Viewport: once: true
- Stagger: 0.1s increments
